Our story

Local Roofers Who Actually Show Up.

Atlas Roofing & Restoration was founded with a clear mission: provide Northeast Ohio homeowners and businesses with an honest, reliable roofing contractor that delivers what it promises.


We're not a national franchise or a call center that subs work out to random crews. When you hire Atlas, you get experienced local roofers based right here in Beachwood, Ohio — people who know our climate, our neighborhoods, and our customers.


From a single shingle repair to a full commercial roof replacement, we approach every job with the same commitment: use the right materials, do the work properly, and make sure you're 100% satisfied before we leave.

🎯
Honesty First

We tell you what you actually need — not the most expensive option. If a repair will do the job, we'll tell you. We earn trust by being straight with people.

⚡
Quality Workmanship

We use premium materials — primarily GAF, the largest roofing manufacturer in North America — and install them to manufacturer specs every time.

🤝
We Stand Behind Our Work

Every project we complete is backed by our workmanship warranty plus manufacturer warranties. If something isn't right, we make it right.

📍
Locally Rooted

We live and work in Northeast Ohio. We're invested in our community and treat every customer's home with the same care we'd want for our own.
