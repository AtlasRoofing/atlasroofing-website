GAF System Plus Limited Warranty — What It Covers and How to Get It

The GAF System Plus Limited Warranty is an enhanced manufacturer warranty that covers both GAF roofing materials AND the installation labor for defects. It is only available when a GAF Certified, Certified Plus, or Master Elite contractor installs a qualifying combination of GAF products. Atlas Roofing & Restoration (GAF ID: 1148301) is a 2026 GAF Certified contractor and can offer this warranty on qualifying installations.

The three tiers of GAF warranty coverage

GAF offers three levels of warranty protection, and understanding the difference matters before you choose a contractor. The chart below shows exactly what each tier covers — pay particular attention to the difference between the basic Shingle & Accessory warranty (which any homeowner gets automatically) and the System Plus warranty (which requires certified installation).

GAF Shingle & Accessory Limited Warranty

This is the baseline warranty that comes automatically with any GAF shingle purchase, regardless of who installs it. It covers manufacturing defects in the shingles themselves. It does not cover installation errors, labor costs, or other system components. If a shingle fails due to a manufacturing defect, GAF replaces the materials. If the shingles were installed incorrectly, you're on your own.

GAF Roofing System Limited Warranty

A step up — this requires installation of GAF Lifetime Shingles plus at least three qualifying accessory products (starter strip, ridge cap, underlayment, etc.). Like the basic warranty, it covers material defects but still does not require certified installation — any contractor can potentially offer this if the right products are used together.

GAF System Plus Limited Warranty

The System Plus is the warranty that matters. It requires installation of Timberline shingles plus at least three qualifying accessory products AND must be installed by a GAF Certified, Certified Plus, or Master Elite contractor. The critical difference is that the System Plus covers both material defects AND workmanship — meaning if the contractor made an installation error that causes a failure, the warranty covers it. This is the warranty Atlas Roofing & Restoration offers on qualifying installations.

Important note on Atlas Roofing's certification: We are a 2026 GAF Certified™ Residential Roofing Contractor (ID: 1148301), which qualifies us to offer the GAF System Plus Limited Warranty. We do not hold Master Elite status, which would additionally allow the Golden Pledge warranty. Any Atlas Roofing installation quote that references the GAF warranty refers specifically to the System Plus Limited Warranty.

What does the System Plus warranty actually cover?

The System Plus covers manufacturing defects in qualifying GAF products and installation workmanship errors for the coverage period. The specific terms — duration, coverage amounts, transferability — vary by product and are detailed in the full warranty document. Atlas Roofing provides the warranty documentation with every qualifying installation so you have the exact terms in writing.

Is the warranty transferable?

GAF System Plus warranties are transferable to a subsequent homeowner within the coverage period, typically for a nominal transfer fee. This is a meaningful selling point when listing your home — a documented, transferable manufacturer warranty is something buyers' agents and home inspectors recognize.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the GAF System Plus Limited Warranty?
The GAF System Plus Limited Warranty is an enhanced coverage that protects against both GAF material defects AND contractor installation workmanship errors. It requires installation by a GAF Certified contractor using qualifying GAF products together as a system.
What's the difference between GAF Certified and Master Elite?
GAF Certified contractors can offer the System Plus Limited Warranty. GAF Master Elite contractors — a higher tier requiring additional training and performance standards — can also offer the Golden Pledge warranty, which provides broader coverage. Atlas Roofing is GAF Certified, not Master Elite.
Does every Atlas Roofing installation get a System Plus warranty?
The System Plus warranty requires a qualifying combination of GAF products. Atlas Roofing installs the full GAF system — Timberline shingles, GAF underlayment, starter strip, and ridge cap — on every job, which qualifies for the System Plus. We document the warranty with every installation.
How long does the GAF System Plus warranty last?
The duration depends on the specific products used. Timberline HDZ shingles carry a Lifetime limited warranty as part of the full system. The specific coverage terms, including any prorated periods, are detailed in the warranty document provided at installation.
What voids a GAF warranty?
Common issues that can void GAF warranties include installation by a non-certified contractor, improper ventilation that causes premature shingle failure, physical damage from storms or foot traffic, and product modifications. Atlas Roofing's installations follow GAF specifications exactly to protect warranty eligibility.
