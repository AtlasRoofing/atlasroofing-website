Denied Or Underpaid?

A "no" from your insurer isn't always the final answer.

Many denials happen because the original inspection missed damage, used the wrong language, or under-priced the repair. We've helped homeowners across Cleveland get reconsidered — here's what we look for:

!
Hidden hail bruising Adjusters routinely miss soft hail damage that compromises shingle integrity over time.
!
Wind-lifted shingles Even shingles that lay flat may have lost their seal — only verifiable with a lift test.
!
Ohio matching law Ohio requires insurers to match replacement materials. If they're only paying for a partial roof, you may be entitled to more.
!
Code-required upgrades Ice & water shield, drip edge, ventilation upgrades — often required by code but missed by adjusters.
