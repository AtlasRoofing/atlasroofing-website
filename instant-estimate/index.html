What happens next

One of our team members will reach out within 2 business hours to schedule a free, no-pressure on-site inspection. A precise quote can only be done in person because it depends on:

  • Condition of your decking and any needed repairs
  • Flashing, vents, and chimney work
  • Ventilation upgrades (ridge vent, soffit intake)
  • Material selection — GAF Timberline HDZ vs. designer shingles
  • Tear-off vs. overlay (we always recommend tear-off)

The good news: insurance claims are our specialty. If hail or wind damage caused this, you may only pay your deductible. We handle the claim for you.
