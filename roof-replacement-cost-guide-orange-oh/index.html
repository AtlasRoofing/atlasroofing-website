What does roof replacement cost in Orange?

Most residential roof replacements in Orange range from $8,000 to $20,000 depending on home size, roof complexity, and material selection. A standard 2,000 sqft ranch with architectural shingles typically falls in the $9,000–$13,000 range. Larger homes, steeper pitches, multiple stories, and complex rooflines all increase cost. Common Questions

What's the average cost of a new roof in Orange, Ohio?
Most {n} homes fall in the $9,000–$16,000 range for a full asphalt shingle replacement. Smaller ranches can be under $9,000; larger two-story colonials with complex rooflines can exceed $18,000. Material selection, pitch, and any needed repairs affect the final number.
What drives up roof replacement cost?
The main cost drivers are: roof size (measured in squares — 100 sqft each), pitch (steep roofs cost more to work on safely), number of layers to remove, decking replacement needed, chimney or skylight flashing, and material tier selected. We identify all of these during our free inspection.
Why are some quotes so much lower than others?
Low quotes often mean one of three things: lower quality materials, skipped underlayment or ice & water shield, or no permit. Any contractor who can't explain exactly what's in their quote should be viewed with caution. Always ask for itemized quotes.
