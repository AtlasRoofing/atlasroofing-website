Roofing in Willoughby Hills — What We Know

Most homes in Willoughby Hills were built during the 1960s–1990s era — primarily colonial and ranch homes. Roofs installed during the first or second replacement cycle in the 1990s and 2000s are now reaching or past their expected 25–30 year lifespan.

Quiet residential suburb between Lyndhurst and Willoughby. Many colonial and ranch homes from the 1970s–80s. Strong upgrade and replacement market.
