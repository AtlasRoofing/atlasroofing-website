How do I know if I need a replacement vs. a repair?+
If your roof is under 15 years old with isolated damage, a repair is often the right move. If it's 15–20+ years old, has widespread shingle damage, multiple leaks, or significant storm damage, a full replacement is usually more cost-effective. We'll give you our honest recommendation during your free inspection — no upselling.
How long does a roof replacement take?+
Most residential roof replacements are completed in a single day. Larger homes or complex rooflines may take two days. We give you a clear timeline upfront so you can plan accordingly.
Will my homeowner's insurance cover a new roof?+
It depends on the cause. Storm damage, hail, and wind are typically covered. Wear and tear from age usually is not. We inspect your roof, document any storm or hail damage, and work directly with your insurance adjuster to maximize your claim. Many customers pay only their deductible.
What roofing materials do you use?+
We install GAF shingles as our primary residential product — GAF is the largest roofing manufacturer in North America, and as a certified installer we offer their Lifetime Warranty. We also work with Mule-Hide and other commercial-grade materials for flat and commercial roofs.
What does the free inspection include?+
Our free inspection is a full professional assessment — shingles, flashing, ventilation, gutters, and any areas of concern. We take photos, document everything, and give you a clear report. Zero obligation to use our services afterward.
Is Atlas Roofing licensed and insured?+
Yes — Atlas Roofing & Restoration is fully licensed, bonded, and insured in Ohio. We're also BBB A+ accredited and GAF certified. You're fully protected on every project.
