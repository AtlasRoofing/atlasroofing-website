When should you replace vs. repair a flat roof in Shaker Heights?

If your Shaker Heights flat roof is under 15 years old with isolated damage, targeted repairs are usually cost-effective. If it's over 15–20 years old, has widespread membrane degradation, multiple leak points, or failing seams throughout, replacement is typically more economical than continued repairs. We give honest assessments — we don't recommend replacement when repair will do.

Common Questions

How much does flat roof replacement cost in Shaker Heights?
Flat roof replacement cost depends on the membrane type selected, roof size, existing roof condition (tear-off vs. recover), and drainage requirements. TPO is typically the most cost-effective single-ply option. We provide free written estimates after inspecting your {n} property.
Can you install over my existing flat roof?
In some cases yes — a recover system can be installed over an existing membrane if the deck is structurally sound and moisture levels are acceptable. We assess this during our free inspection. Recover systems save disposal costs but aren't appropriate in every situation.
How long does flat roof replacement take?
Most commercial flat roof replacements in {n} can be completed in 1–5 days depending on size. We plan installation sequencing to minimize disruption to building operations and provide clear scheduling before work begins.
What flat roof system lasts longest?
TPO, EPDM, and PVC all achieve 20–30 year lifespans when properly installed and maintained. PVC and EPDM have the longest real-world track records. The quality of installation matters as much as the membrane choice.
