FAQs

Common Questions

What makes GAF Timberline HDZ different from standard Timberline?
The HDZ features LayerLock technology — a built-in locking mechanism that joins adjacent shingles together, dramatically increasing wind resistance. It also has a wider nailing zone and improved SureNail technology for more consistent installation.
What warranty comes with Timberline HDZ?
Timberline HDZ carries a Lifetime limited warranty on materials. When installed by a GAF certified contractor like Atlas Roofing, it qualifies for the GAF System Plus Limited Warranty — which adds coverage for installation workmanship.
What's the WindProven warranty?
WindProven is GAF's unlimited wind speed warranty available when the complete GAF roofing system is installed together — HDZ shingles, starter strip, ridge cap, and qualifying underlayment. Atlas Roofing installs the full GAF system on every job.
What Timberline HDZ colors are available?
The most popular Timberline HDZ colors in Gates Mills are Charcoal, Weathered Wood, Pewter Gray, Barkwood, and Shakewood. We bring samples to every estimate so you can see the colors against your home's siding and trim before committing.
