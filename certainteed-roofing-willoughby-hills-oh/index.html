Frequently Asked Questions

Does Atlas Roofing install CertainTeed shingles in Willoughby Hills?
Yes — Atlas Roofing & Restoration installs CertainTeed shingles throughout Willoughby Hills and Lake County. We'll help you choose the right CertainTeed product for your home, budget, and desired warranty coverage.
What is the CertainTeed Landmark shingle?
The CertainTeed Landmark is a dual-layered architectural shingle that provides a dimensional wood-shake look. It's available in dozens of colors, carries a Lifetime limited warranty, and is one of the most widely installed shingles in Northeast Ohio.
What warranty comes with CertainTeed shingles?
CertainTeed's Landmark shingles carry a Lifetime limited warranty against manufacturing defects and a 10-year SureStart PLUS warranty that covers 100% of labor and material costs for the first 10 years with a certified installation.
How does CertainTeed compare to GAF?
Both are excellent products. CertainTeed typically offers a wider range of colors and aesthetic options. GAF's Timberline HDZ has the industry-leading LayerLock technology and WindProven wind resistance. We install both and are happy to do a side-by-side comparison during your free estimate.
