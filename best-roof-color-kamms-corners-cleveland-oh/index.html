Frequently Asked Questions

What roof color looks best on Kamms Corners homes?
It depends on your siding color and home style. For Kamms Corners's common brick colonials, Charcoal or Weathered Wood are perennial favorites. For homes with grey or white siding, Pewter Gray or Shakewood offer clean coordination. We bring samples to every estimate — seeing them against your actual home is the only reliable way to choose.
Does roof color affect home value?
Neutral, classic colors that coordinate with the home's exterior tend to photograph better and appeal to the widest range of buyers. Unusual or mismatched colors can actually hurt resale value. When in doubt, Charcoal is the safest choice for Northeast Ohio homes.
Does roof color affect energy efficiency?
Lighter colors reflect more heat, which can reduce summer cooling costs. Darker colors absorb more heat, which can help in winter but increase summer cooling load. For most Ohio homes the difference is modest, but if energy efficiency is a priority, GAF's Timberline Cool Series offers solar-reflective options in several popular colors.
Can I see shingle samples before deciding?
Absolutely — we bring GAF shingle samples to every estimate for Kamms Corners homeowners. You can also request samples to keep while you decide. We never rush the color selection process.
