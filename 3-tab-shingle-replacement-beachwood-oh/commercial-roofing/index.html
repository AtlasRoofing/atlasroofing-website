Roofing systems

We Install Every Major Commercial System

From flat roofs to low-slope commercial buildings — we recommend the right system for your property and install it to manufacturer specs.

TPO
TPO (Thermoplastic Polyolefin)

The most popular commercial flat roofing system. Energy efficient, highly reflective, and excellent at resisting UV, chemical exposure, and punctures. Ideal for most flat commercial roofs in Northeast Ohio.

Most popular flat roof
EPDM
EPDM (Rubber Roofing)

A proven, durable rubber membrane ideal for flat and low-slope roofs. Excellent weathering resistance and a 20–30 year lifespan. Cost-effective and easy to maintain and repair.

Cost-effective flat roof
METAL
Commercial Metal Roofing

Standing seam and R-panel metal roofing for commercial buildings. 40–60 year lifespan, exceptional durability, and great for buildings needing both performance and aesthetics.

Premium longevity
MOD BIT
Modified Bitumen

A multi-layer asphalt system offering outstanding waterproofing and durability. Great for flat roofs on commercial buildings and multi-family properties. Tried and tested for decades.

Common questions

Commercial Roofing FAQs

Answers to the questions we hear most from Northeast Ohio property owners and managers.

How often should I have my commercial roof inspected?+
We recommend at least twice a year — once in spring and once in fall. Additionally, after any significant weather event like hail or high winds. Regular inspections catch small issues before they become expensive repairs and help keep your warranty valid.
What's the best commercial roofing system for Northeast Ohio?+
TPO is our most recommended system for flat commercial roofs — it's energy efficient, handles Cleveland's weather well, and offers excellent value. For buildings needing longevity above all else, metal roofing is hard to beat. We'll recommend the right system based on your building's specific requirements.
Can you repair my roof without replacing it?+
Absolutely. Many commercial roofing issues can be resolved with targeted repairs rather than full replacement. We'll give you an honest assessment during your free inspection and only recommend replacement when it's genuinely the most cost-effective long-term solution.
Do you work on multi-tenant and apartment buildings?+
Yes — we specialize in multi-family roofing and have experience managing large-scale projects with minimal disruption to tenants. We handle everything from documentation to tenant communication coordination.
Will my commercial property insurance cover roof damage?+
Storm damage, hail, and wind damage to commercial roofs is typically covered by commercial property insurance. We inspect, document, and work directly with your insurer to maximize your claim. We've helped many property owners get full replacements covered by insurance.
